## Related Licenses

### Similar Licenses
- **Apache 2.0**: Like MIT but includes explicit patent grant
- **BSD-2-Clause**: Simpler than MIT, nearly identical
- **GPL v3**: Requires sharing modifications (Copyleft)
- **Unlicense**: Public domain equivalent

### Choosing Between Them
- **MIT**: Best for libraries, maximum freedom
- **Apache 2.0**: Good patent protection
- **GPL**: Best for open-source enforcement
- **Unlicense**: Maximum freedom for recipients
